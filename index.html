FriendTrip

"I thought you brought it when we left, now we are in the middle of nowhere and have nothing to drink!"

FriendTrip can help you plan and prepare for your trips much more efficiently by allowing you put all destinations in this trip and all items you need for this trip together.

EasyCook

"I want to learn cooking, but where should I start?"

EasyCook allows you to choose recipes based on what you have in your fridge. It also offers a step-by-step tutorial on each recipe so that everyone can learn and enjoy cooking.

MuseBlend

"I enjoy listening to my list of songs, but I want some new songs."

MuseBlend can help you find your new favorite songs. You can choose what features you want from several songs and our system will tell you which songs have the most similar features.
