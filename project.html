PROJECT

I have work on many different projects, covering topics including web development and machine learning. I have built a few web apps with together with others from scratch and integrate machine learning techniques in some of them.

FriendTrip

FriendTrip is a trip organizing app. With FriendTrip, you can plan a trip together with your friends, and it will also help you keep track of trip-related information such as expenses or destinations. It is hosted on heorku and supported by the database provided by firebase. Multiple users can create accounts and plan the same trip together by editing the items or expenses needed for this trip.

EasyCook

EasyCook helps you to learn cooking. It can help you determine what food you can make based on what ingredients you have in your fridge. It also has a timer function which can help you during cooking.
